Vaastu is an ancient Indian architectural science that focuses on creating harmony between nature, the five elements, and the spaces where we live and work. By balancing the energies associated with direction, light, air, water, and space, Vaastu aims to create environments that support health, happiness, prosperity, and overall well-being. Rather than relying solely on structural changes, Vaastu emphasizes thoughtful planning and practical adjustments that promote positive energy and a balanced lifestyle.
